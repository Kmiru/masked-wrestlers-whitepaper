# Platform (Your daily loop: fight → level → evolve)

Masked Wrestlers is built to hit that perfect competitive rhythm:
**jump in fast, learn forever, and always have a reason to run it back.**

---

## Your player loop

### Step 1 — Equip your identity
- Choose your **Mask** (your persona + tier foundation)
- Slot your **Patches** (skills/modifiers) where available
- Complete the look with **Ring Gear** (Boots/Tights/Top/Cape)

### Step 2 — Enter the arena
- Ranked sets to climb
- Casual sets to experiment
- Training/PvE (as supported) to sharpen fundamentals

### Step 3 — Earn progression
- XP → **Level 1–1000**
- Milestones unlock deeper build expression
- Your build evolves as your skill evolves

### Step 4 — Refine and specialize
Over time you become a recognizable type of threat:
- pressure specialist
- control and punish player
- defense-to-counter monster
- movement and mixup artist

---

## The platform promise
- Fast entry
- Deep mastery
- High replay value
- Competitive integrity from day one

This platform is designed for the players who want a game to grind for years.
