# Platform

Masked Wrestlers is designed to be:
- easy to enter
- hard to master
- rewarding to grind

## Player flow
1) Equip your Mask
2) Build with Patches and Ring Gear
3) Fight → earn XP → level up
4) Unlock milestones and expand build access
5) Refine your identity across seasons

The platform is built for both:
- daily ladder grinders
- style collectors
- build theorycrafters
- highlight hunters
