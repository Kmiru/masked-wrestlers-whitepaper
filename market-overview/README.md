# Masked Wrestlers

Masked Wrestlers is a **skill-first competitive fighting game** where **your hands, your reads, and your timing** decide the winner.

It combines the intensity of competitive PvP with the culture of masked personas, letting you build a fighter identity through:
- **Masks** (your core persona)
- **Mask Accessories** (skills/modifiers)
- **Ring Gear** (boots, tights, top, cape)


---

## Why Masked Wrestlers

### A competitive game you can take seriously
Masked Wrestlers is designed around what competitive players care about:
- fair ranked ladders
- mastery and matchup depth
- clear counterplay
- integrity protections that keep wins meaningful

### An identity you can build and own
Your Mask isn’t just a skin — it’s a persona. Over time, you can craft a signature look and style that feels uniquely yours, season after season.

---

## What you can do

### Fight
- **Ranked PvP:** climb by skill and consistency  
- **Casual PvP:** experiment, warm up, run sets  
- **Training / PvE (where available):** sharpen fundamentals and learn matchups

### Progress
- earn **XP → Level 1–1000**
- unlock milestones that expand build options over time
- grow from beginner to specialist through repetition and mastery

### Customize
Your loadout is built from:
1) **1 Mask**
2) **Mask Accessories** (skills/modifiers)
3) **Ring Gear** (boots / tights / top / cape)


---

## The player first

Masked Wrestlers protects competitive integrity through:
- **global caps** so stats can’t spiral
- **tradeoffs + counterplay** so every advantage has answers
- **tier / slot rules** to keep builds structured
- **anti-exploit protections** (equip lock/escrow + match-bound loadout verification)

## NFTs are powerful

### If you play without NFTs
You still get the core game:
- fight, learn, climb
- level up
- develop your style
- compete on fair rules

### If you choose NFTs (optional)
NFTs give you:

- **collection goals** through seasonal themes and culture drops
