# Patches and seasonal supply

Mask Accessories are designed to be a “sink + refresh” system so the market does not dry out.

## Season 0 example supply (launch year)

A practical starting point:

- Total: 25,000 accessories
- Common: 70%
- Rare: 25%
- Legendary: 5%

## Seasonal replenishment

Each season (e.g., every 3 months) can introduce 5,000–12,000 new accessories depending on demand and burn rate.
