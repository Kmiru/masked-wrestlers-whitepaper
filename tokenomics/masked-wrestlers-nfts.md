# Masked Wrestlers NFTs

## Masks (Genesis)

- Fixed supply Genesis collection.
- Tiers: Common / Rare / Legendary.
- Slots scale by tier (1 / 2 / 3).

## Mask Accessories (seasonal)

Mask Accessories can be issued by seasons to maintain liquidity and let the meta evolve.

They are tradable only with:

- escrow/lock while equipped,
- tier restrictions,
- slot rules + level gates.
